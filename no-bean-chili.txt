No Bean Chili

Prep time: 30 minutes
Cook time: 2 hours

Ingredients:
- 32.4 ounces (1 liter) beef brone broth
- 3 pounds ground beef
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 1/2 tablespoon cumin
- 6 chopped onions
- 2 teaspoon Worcester sauce
- 4 cloves garlic minced
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 4 tablespoons chili powder
- 1/2 tablespoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon red pepper
- 1 teaspoon all spice
- 12 ounces tomato paste
- 6 bay leaves
- 1/2 cup chili peppers finely chopped

Instructions:
- Brown the beef and keep the beef fat.
- Caramelize the chopped onions.
- Add remaining ingredients, bring to a boil, then simmer about 2 hours.

Toppings:
- Sour cream
- Shredded Gouda
