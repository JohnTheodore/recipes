Cauliflower Hash Browns

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Passive Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:
- 3 cups grated cauliflower
- 2 large eggs
- 3/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp black pepper

Instructions:
- Microwave grated cauliflower.
- Place cauliflower in paper towels or cheese cloth and ring out all excess water.
- Place rung out cauliflower in a bowl, add rest of ingredients and combine well.
- Form into 6 square shaped hash browns on a greased baking tray.
- Place in 400 degree oven for 15-20 minutes.
- Let cool for 10 minutes and hash browns will firm up.
