Herb Roasted Lamb Loin Chops

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 6 + 10 + 5 minutes

Ingredients:
- 4 large garlic cloves, pressed
- 1/2 tablespoon thyme leaves
- 1/2 tablespoon rosemary leaves
- 2 teaspoons kosher salt
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 5 1.25-inch-thick lamb loin chops (about 1 pound)

Instructions:
- Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
- Mix garlic, thyme, rosemary, and olive oil in large bowl.
- Add lamb; turn to coat.
- Let marinate at room temperature at least 30 minutes and up to 1 hour.
- Heat olive oil in heavy large ovenproof skillet over high heat.
- Add lamb; cook until browned, about 3 minutes per side.
- Transfer skillet to oven and roast lamb chops to desired doneness, about 10 minutes for medium-rare.
- Transfer lamb to platter, cover, and let rest for 5 minutes.
