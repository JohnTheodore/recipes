Roast Duck Breast

Ingredients:
- Duck breasts with skin on.

Preparation:
- Put the duck breast on dry paper towels skin side up in the fridge to dry for 4 days. (this lets the skin crisp in the pan better later)

Instructions:
- Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
- Score the duck breast skin with knife in cross-hatch pattern.
- Salt and pepper both sides of duck breast.
- Place the duck breast skin side down in a pan on high heat for a few minutes until golden brown and crispy 
- Place duck breast in oven for 7 minutes.
