Chocolate Avocado Pudding

Ingredients:
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt
- 1 tablespoon of vanilla
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1/4 cup of honey
- 3 ripe avocados (they must be ripe!)
- 1/2 cocoa powder

Instructions:
- mix all the ingredients (melt the honey if necessary)
- immersion blend it all, it should be smooth and fluffy
- if you don't include milk or heavy cream, the avocados won't get smooth on their own
